Hi! I'm Maylo, a 20-year-old software developer with a strong passion for front-end development and game creation. I love bringing ideas to life through clean code and creative design. Front-end development really sparked my interest in the visual and interactive side of software, and it's where I get to combine my technical skills with creativity. Over the years, I’ve gained a wide range of experience—from building websites with HTML/CSS, and JavaScript to creating 3D and 2D games. I also enjoy building PCs and exploring the more hands-on, hardware side of tech. Whether I’m coding a game, bringing interfaces to life with front-end code, or exploring new tools, I’m always eager to grow and create.
